Get to know the cast

Cast

Allison Akmajian
Anja

Born and raised in Tucson, Allison Akmajian was cast as Winthrop in THE MUSIC MAN at age 7 and hasn’t stopped performing since. She has appeared as Portia in THE MERCHANT OF VENICE, Jo in INDEPENDENCE, and King Duncan in MACBETH. She has studied with the Oregon Shakespeare Festival, RADA, and has recently graduated from the Three Year Professional Conservatory at The Stella Adler Studio of Acting. Keep up with Allison on Instagram @akmajian or allisonakmajian.com

Julian Alexander
Joze

Julian Alexander was born and raised in Miami, Florida. He attended college at Loyola Marymount University where he was fortunate enough to work on many wonderful plays, such as, References to Salvador Dali Make Me Hot, Honey Brown Eyes, Someone Who'll Watch Over Me, The Bacchae and so on. He was also fortunate enough to study abroad at the Moscow Art Theatre School in Moscow, Russia during his time in school. Since then, he has most recently appeared in CBS's Blue Bloods, TV One's Merry Wishmas along with a co-star part in a 20th Fox Century Pilot Fantasy Life and the west coast premier of the play A Couple of Poor Polish Speaking Romanians. His heroes tend to be physicists and he one day hopes to be able to play the trumpet proficiently. Thank you all for coming to our show!

​Dante Jayce
Zol

Dante is thrilled to be part of “Owl Girl.” Recent NYC Theatre: Daisy in Baby with the Bathwater (Roaring Epiphany/Medicine Show Theatre), Claudius in Hamlet (Alpha NYC/Producers Club), Antonio in The Tempest (EPIC Players/Flea Theater) Alquist in R.U.R. (Battle Damage/Players Theatre). Film/TV: The Jon Tron Show, La Cible, We Just Met, The Raging Elephant. Awards include: Audience Choice Award at the 2017 SPF Boo Festival for Masque of the Red Death, Best Ensemble Performance at the 2016 Planet Connections Festival for Helvetica, and being named one of the “brighter talents” at the 2012 NY International Fringe Festival for Nocturnal. “The Ace” is a fixture on the independent wrestling circuit and his interviewing talents were utilized as host of the 2017 Big Apple Comic Con. Dante thanks Melissa, his Momma, his friends, and everyone involved in helping create this beautiful show. Subscribe to Dante’s YouTube channel and follow him on Instagram/Twitter (@DanteJayce).

Mariam Jibladze
​Capi

Mariam Jibladze is extremely proud to be making her professional stage debut with THML Theater Company.  She is in sixth grade and loves her school. Mariam enjoys dance, playing piano and the guitar. She has been singing and acting since she was 4 at her school theater and junior off-Broadway musicals. Mariam feels especially happy when she is on the stage and has a dream to be a Broadway star. She thanks THML Theater Company for this opportunity and also her family who had always cared and helped her dream big dreams. To get to know her more check out her instagram @mariam_j_official.

Robert Resnikoff
Rav

ROBERT RESNIKOFF created the role of Igor Stravinsky in the world premiere of Frederick Stroppel’s Small World with the Stray Katz Theater Company in Newtown, CT, and repeated the role with the White Heron Theatre Company on Nantucket. Other stage roles have included Jack in Brighton Beach Memoirs,Adrian in Private Eyes, Claudius in Hamlet, Dr. Lyman Hall in 1776, and Hersh in the premiere of Brian C. Petti’s Masquerade. On screen he has played a psychotic network TV news anchor in Crumbling Walls, a group therapy leader in Father and the Stolichnaya Master Distiller in a promotional video for the vodka company. Robert has presented classical music on WQXR-NY and on WSHU-Fairfield. Voice-over work has included the Connecticut Public Television documentary East of the River and the audiobook Emigration. He has taught Acting Shakespeare in Wesleyan University’s Graduate Liberal Studies Program and directed the Oddfellows Playhouse Summer Shakespeare Academy.

Yaara Shilony
​Stel

Yaara Shilony is an Israeli actress, singer and costumes designer based in Brooklyn. Yaara moved to New York in order to study at AMDA and pursue an acting career. She recently shot her first feature film and made her off Broadway debut. 
She is very excited to be a part of such passionate and creative group of people. ​​
